FACIAL REANIMATION AFTER ACOUSTIC NEUROMA RESECTION WITH INJURY TO THE

This young lady had 2 attemps to restore a spontaneous smile . Only a small strip of temporalis muscle was used to achieve eye closure . Notice the absence of visible scars in her face and the spontaneity in her behaveour and smile
