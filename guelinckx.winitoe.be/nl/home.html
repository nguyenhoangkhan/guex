Specialisaties

Prof Paul Guelinckx had a broad basic training in plastic reconstructive and aesthetic surgery under guidance of PROF M LEJOUR . His interest in facial reanimation started at the University of LEUVEN where he did all his research on muscle transplantation and nerve repair . This resulted in a Ph.D. thesis in 1987 under Prof JOHN FAULKNER a world leader in striated muscle phisiology. Futheron his endeaver was alays to improve the clinical outcome of patients with a facial palsy.He started his clinical pratice at The CATHOLIC UNIVERSITY HOSPITAL Gasthuisberg and continued his activity from 2001 in JESSA HOSPITAL HASSELT campus salvator .
